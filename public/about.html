About

I'm Georgia and I'm from from London, but also spent part of my childhood growing up in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Since graduating from the University of Bristol in 2016 with a degree in History, I've travelled, volunteered at the Rio Olympics and worked on a cruise ship.

When I'm not at a computer, you can find me at the theatre, practising my crow pose, or perhaps teaching my Scout Troop to code.
