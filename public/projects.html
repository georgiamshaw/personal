Learning Projects

Every week we build a small, one page project that demonstrates our understanding of the topic we have been focusing on that week. Each project is built by a team of four students, and we spend a day and a half building, and another half a day code reviewing other teams, fixing bugs and issues, and presenting our work to the rest of the students.
